Yet another setup tool?

YaST is the installation and configuration tool for openSUSE and the SUSE Linux Enterprise distributions. It is popular for its attractive and easy to use graphical interface and the capability to customize your system quickly during and after the installation. YaST stands for Yet Another Setup Tool, but it's much more than that. YaST is actually the most versatile and comprehensive installation and configuration tool for Linux systems.
